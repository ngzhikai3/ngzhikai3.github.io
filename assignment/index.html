ALL-STAR GAME

The West's Kobe Bryant and Shaquille O'Neal were named joint winners of the All-Star Game Most Valuable Player Award.

The 2009 NBA All-Star Game was the 58th edition of the NBA All-Star Game, and was played at the US Airways Center in Phoenix, Arizona. The Western Conference defeated the Eastern Conference, 146-119.

The 2016 NBA All-Star Game was the 65th edition of the NBA All-Star Game, and was played at Air Canada Centre in Toronto. Russell Westbrook was named the All-Star Game Most Valuable Player. This was also the 18th and final All-Star Game in which Kobe Bryant participated, as a result of his retirement after the season.

The Western Conference defeated the Eastern Conference, 196-173.

The 2019 NBA All-Star Game was the 68th edition of the NBA All-Star Game, and was played at the Spectrum Center in Charlotte, North Carolina.

The 2020 NBA All-Star Game was the 69th edition of the NBA All-Star Game, and was played at the United Center in Chicago.

Kevin Durant collects MVP honors after he helps Team LeBron rally from a 20-point hole to topple Team Giannis, 178-164.

The 2022 NBA All-Star Game was the 71st edition of the NBA All-Star Game. With teams captained by LeBron James and Kevin Durant, Team LeBron won the game 163-160.
