About

Take home a photo of you surfing in-between mountains, above the arctic circle, in the most beautiful place in the world!

Unstad Arctic Surf – Beginner Surf Lesson with Photographer.

Our Photographer

We have a photographer who has been freelancing for over 10 years and specialises in surf photography. Our photographer is there to capture your whole experience at Unstad Arctic Surf. Take home life long memories of you surfing in-between mountains, above the arctic circle, in the most beautiful place in the world!

Lessons

Learn the basic surfing fundamentals in a step-by-step surf lesson taught by certified instructors at Unstad Arctic Surf. Unstad is the perfect backdrop to learn with friendly waves, whilst the more advanced surfers can enjoy world class waves on the surf breaks further out.

We pride ourselves on our surf lessons and are teaching methods guarantee success!

Surf Lessons

• Daily surf lessons 7 day’s a week, 365 days a year
• Qualified and experienced instructors
• Latest wetsuits and surfboards
• Modern indoor changing rooms
• Hot shower facilities for after your surf
• Enjoy our brand new sauna post surf
• Access to ALL photos taken by our professional photographer

Surf FAQ

Join Our Morning or Afternoon Session. Please note lesson times are tide dependant and can change. We will comfirm the times via email or text with you.

If its just you and a few friends you will be fine. If you are a large group, then you may want to call us to make sure we have everything ready when you come in.

Price is 1690 NOK Per Person.

We serve a large breakfast buffet in the cafe/restaurant area. During the high season we serve breakfast every morning from 9-10am. During the low season it is upon request. Speak to one of our friendly staff to book your breakfast.

The temperatures are between 11 to 14°C (52 to 59°F) towards the end of the summer months and are at their lowest during April, in the range of 6 to 8°C (43 to 47°F).
